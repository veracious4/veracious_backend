(Chutian Metropolis Daily/screen grab)Latest figures from the National Health Commission on the spread of the virus showed 24 new cases across China, and 22 more deaths as of Tuesday.
 All of the latest deaths occurred in Wuhan.However new infections in the wider Hubei province continue to stabilise, with new cases declining for the sixth day.
